Founder

Satyavathi Barla

Satyavathi's journey led her to create WINZ MEDIA, aiming to empower the younger generation and foster digital creativity. We believe impactful video storytelling should be accessible to all businesses, regardless of size or budget.

Passion drives us at WINZ MEDIA. Satyavathi's dedication shines through in our women-centric channel, which amplifies diverse and inspiring stories of women to motivate and inspire others.

DOP and working partner

Kolli Durga Prasad

Durga Prasad began his filmmaking journey with Trends A D Film Maker Pvt Ltd in Bangalore, honing his skills from 2000 to 2002. His passion for cinematography then led him to Mumbai, where he spent a decade as an Assistant Cinematographer under the esteemed Mr. P S Vinod. Renowned for his work in big-budget Telugu projects, Mr. Vinod provided invaluable mentorship, shaping Durga Prasad into the accomplished professional he is today.
