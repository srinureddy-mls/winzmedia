  3. Testimonial Videos: Let your satisfied customers do the talking! We create authentic testimonial videos that showcase the positive experiences of your clients, helping to build trust and credibility for your brand.

  6. Product Shoot: Highlight the best features of your products with professional product photography and videography. Whether it's for e-commerce websites or promotional materials, we ensure your products shine.

Durga Prasad Kolli

Meet Durga Prasad Kolli, our talented Cinematographer, bringing years of experience and expertise to the WINZ MEDIA team.

Durga Prasad embarked on his journey in the world of filmmaking with Trends A D Film Maker Pvt Ltd, based in Bangalore, where he honed his skills for two years from 2000 to 2002. His passion for cinematography led him to Mumbai, where he spent a decade as an Assistant Cinematographer under the mentorship of the esteemed Mr. P S Vinod. Mr. Vinod, renowned for his work in big-budget Telugu projects, imparted invaluable knowledge and experience to Durga Prasad, shaping him into the accomplished professional he is today.

With a rich background in cinematography, Durga Prasad brings to the table a wealth of experience, training, and a proven track record of success. His expertise spans across all facets of film production, including camera lens selection, camera operation, lighting design, set construction, and leveraging cutting-edge technologies to enhance the visual impact of every project.

Durga Prasad's collaborative spirit and dedication to his craft enable him to effectively prioritize the creative vision of each film while working seamlessly with fellow team members. His commitment to creating visually captivating narratives infused with depth and emotion is evident in every frame he captures.

Driven by a relentless pursuit of perfection and a thirst for continuous learning, Durga Prasad remains at the forefront of his field, constantly evolving with the latest advancements in technology and pushing the boundaries of creativity.

Join us in celebrating Durga Prasad Kolli's passion for storytelling and his unwavering commitment to bringing cinematic visions to life at WINZ MEDIA.
